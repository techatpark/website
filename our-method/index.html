Idea of TECH@PARK was not born in boardroom discussion. We are not backed by any funding. We do not have classrooms or paid instructors. But still we have made a difrence in the quality of talents we produce. Our results are diffrent because our methods itself is different

  • Picking a Candidate

    We identify potential candidates through trusted sources ( i.e. Colleagues/Ex-Students) who know their potentials personally. we then interview certain parameters to see if he has 'IT' in him

  • Team Farming

    Students are expected to stay with the trainer. We encourage them to farm as a team to learn Engineering process. he will then be involved in real world projects which can eigther be Open Source or from a potential hirer.

  • Pro-gramming

    Students are trained on the latest market trends & technologies by industry experts. There is no predefined training duration period. It goes until he or the trainer feels 'COMPLETE'. While joining, our students may not be the same. But they should be when they 'COMP(L)ETE'

  • Placement

    We reach out to companies that need relevant skill sets. As a principle, we never recommend people we just refer to. When both the company and student find themselves matching. Our Student becomes ALUMNI :)
